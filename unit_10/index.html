About Me

I am a Graphic Designer in the Northern VA, Washington DC area. I specialize in graphic and print design, motion graphics, web, and UI design. I specialize in brand, typographic, and layout design, but have a passion for learning new areas of design. I have worked as a freelance designer, marketing designer, and as an in-house designer. I have experience as an in-house designer and have the passion and desire to not only commit to, but to meet tight deadlines while attaining the desire to learn new things.
